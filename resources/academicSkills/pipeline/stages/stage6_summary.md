# Stage 6: SUMMARY

## Purpose
Document the writing process and archive materials

## Agents
- `process_documenter` — Records the writing process
- `material_archiver` — Archives all materials

## Process
1. Process documenter creates summary of all stages
2. Material archiver organizes and stores all materials
3. Quality metrics are recorded
4. Lessons learned are documented

## Inputs
- All outputs from Stages 1-5
- Review reports
- Revision notes

## Outputs
- Process Summary Document
- Archived Materials (sources, drafts, reviews)
- Quality Metrics

## Summary Document Contents

### Writing Process Overview
- Timeline of activities
- Key decisions made
- Challenges encountered
- Solutions implemented

### Quality Metrics
- Initial review score
- Final review score
- Number of revisions
- Citation accuracy

### Materials Archive
- Research sources
- Outlines
- Drafts (all versions)
- Review reports
- Final document

### Lessons Learned
- What worked well
- What could be improved
- Recommendations for future papers

## Quality Gates
- [ ] All stages documented
- [ ] Materials properly archived
- [ ] Quality metrics recorded
- [ ] Lessons learned captured

## User Checkpoint
User confirms:
1. Summary is accurate
2. All materials archived
3. Process is complete

## Mode Differences
- **Academic Mode**: Full documentation, comprehensive archive
- **Engineering Mode**: Minimal documentation, essential materials only
