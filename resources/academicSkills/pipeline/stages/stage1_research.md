# Stage 1: RESEARCH

## Purpose
Gather and verify sources, define research question

## Agents
- `research_coordinator` — Manages the research process
- `source_verifier` — Checks DOI, author, journal legitimacy
- `literature_analyst` — Analyzes and synthesizes literature

## Process
1. User provides research topic
2. Research coordinator suggests search strategy
3. Sources are collected and organized
4. Source verifier checks legitimacy
5. Literature analyst synthesizes findings
6. Research question is refined

## Inputs
- Research topic
- Initial questions
- Existing literature (optional)

## Outputs
- Research Question Brief
- Annotated Bibliography
- Source Verification Report

## Quality Gates
- [ ] Research question is specific and researchable
- [ ] Search strategy is comprehensive
- [ ] All sources verified (DOI exists, journal legitimate)
- [ ] No retracted papers included
- [ ] Coverage is sufficient for topic

## User Checkpoint
User confirms:
1. Research question is correct
2. Sources are relevant
3. No important sources missing

## Mode Differences
- **Academic Mode**: Full verification, comprehensive search
- **Engineering Mode**: Quick search, focus on relevance
